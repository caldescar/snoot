10 Questions

Instructions: Find a partner. Ask your partner the questions below. Record your partner's answers next to the questions.

  • What is your name? Liza.
  • Is a hotdog a sandwich? No
  • Can you wear socks with sandles? Absolutely not.
  • Where do you call home? Fairfax
  • Is cereal soup? No.
  • Is a wafer a cookie? Yes.
  • Do you drive in the parkway or park in the driveway? I park in the driveway.
  • Which goes in the bowl first, the milk or the cereal? I pour cereal first.
  • Subs or dubs? Dubs.
  • Do you like waffles? Yes.
  • If you could be an animal, what animal would you be? A lion.
  • What is the answer to everything? That's just a weird question.
